Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Car Parts operated by Car Parts collects, uses, stores, and deletes user data.

1. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact: sergey.bmx@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect and store the following data:

  • Account data: name, email address, phone number, city, avatar.
  • Listing data: title, description, part number, brand, model, generation, photos, location, price, and other information you add to your listings.
  • Favorites data: products you save to favorites.
  • Technical and service data: authentication status, timestamps, and data required for Firebase, security, and app functionality.

Guest users can browse the catalog without creating an account, but some functionality is restricted until sign-in.

3. How We Use Data

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To publish and display listings in the marketplace.
  • To enable profile editing, favorites, and account management.
  • To provide support, maintain security, and prevent abuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations when applicable.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party infrastructure providers, including:

  • Google Firebase for authentication, database, and storage.
  • Google Sign-In for account login where applicable.

These services may process data as required to operate the app. Their handling of data is governed by their own policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

Some user-provided marketplace data may be visible to other users, including:

  • seller name;
  • listing description and images;
  • city or location;
  • seller contact details when shown as part of listing functionality.

6. Data Retention

We keep account and listing data for as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our terms.

If you request account deletion, we will delete your account and associated data, except where retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

7. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and related data in one of the following ways:

  • through the in-app account deletion feature, if available;
  • through the public deletion request page: Account Deletion;
  • by contacting sergey.bmx@gmail.com.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children

The app is not intended for children unless explicitly stated otherwise. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us at sergey.bmx@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
