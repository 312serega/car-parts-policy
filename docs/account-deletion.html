Account Deletion

If you created an account in Car Parts, you can request deletion of your account and associated data.

Delete from the App

Open the app, sign in to your account, go to your profile, and use the account deletion option if available.

Delete Outside the App

If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request to sergey.bmx@gmail.com.

In your message, include:

  • the phone number or email linked to your account;
  • your request to delete the account;
  • optional information that helps identify your account.

What Will Be Deleted

  • your profile data;
  • your listings, where applicable;
  • your favorites and associated account records;
  • other data directly associated with your account, unless retention is required by law.

Retention Exceptions

Some information may be retained for a limited period where required for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, technical logs, or legal obligations.

Response Time

We aim to review deletion requests within a reasonable time after receipt.
